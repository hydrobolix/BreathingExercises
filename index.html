Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between System 1 and System 2?

System 1 is organized by technique name — each letter maps to a named breathing method (e.g. Box Breathing, Ujjayi, Wim Hof). System 2 is organized by intention or effect — each letter maps to a goal or feeling (e.g. Anxiety Relief, Energy Boost, Stress Release). Use System 1 to learn a method; use System 2 to find what you need right now.

Are these exercises safe for everyone?

Most techniques are gentle and suitable for healthy adults. However, if you have a respiratory condition, cardiovascular issue, or are pregnant, please consult a healthcare professional before practicing advanced breathwork (e.g. Wim Hof, Kapalabhati, or extended breath holds).

How do I use the CSV download?

Click "Download CSV" on either system tab to get a spreadsheet-ready file with all 26 exercises. Great for printing, journaling, or importing into your own apps.

Can I suggest a new technique or correction?

Yes — that's exactly what QCC is for. Click "Open a GitHub Issue" above and describe your suggestion. We review all submissions and credit contributors in the changelog.
